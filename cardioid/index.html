Cardioids

Explanations

On a circle, n points are evenly situated. Leftmost point is numbered 0 and all other points in a clockwise way are numbered consecutively. Each point is picked, its associated number is multiplied by m (multiplier), and then the resulting number is divided by n. Lletting the remainder be r, a line is drawn between the point and point r.

Instructions

  • Press 'run' button to start simulation.
  • Press 'r' to restart simulation.
  • Press 'p' to toggle between pause/resume.
  • You can let muliplier automatically increase by 0.1 or you can pick it for a snapshot.
  • In the latter case, you need to click 'run' button only once. Every time you pick a number, the canvas redraws the result instantly.
